Getting started in 5 steps
1
Import — drag video or audio onto the Browser panel, or click the drop zone. Or open the 🎵 Music tab for built-in tracks.
2
Add to timeline — select a clip in the Bin, press Insert (or drag it to a track). Set Mark In/Out with I / O first to use only part of a clip.
3
Edit — drag clips to reorder. Press B to cut. Resize edges to trim. Press T to add a text title. Press M to multi-select.
4
Polish — select any clip and use the Inspector to add fades, transitions, colour grades (LUTs), motion effects, or volume envelopes.
5
Export — click Export in the toolbar. Pick a device preset (iPhone · YouTube · Instagram…) and click Export →. Save your project with ⌘S any time.
